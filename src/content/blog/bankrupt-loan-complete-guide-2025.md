### **Q4: Boleh negotiate untuk discharged awal tak?**

**A**: **YA, BOLEH!** Beberapa cara:

#### Cara 1: Voluntary Arrangement (VA)

**Apa itu**: Agreement antara you, DGI, dan creditors untuk settle debt dengan reduced amount.

**Process**:
1. Hire insolvency practitioner / peguam
2. Propose settlement amount (usually 30-50% dari total debt)
3. DGI & creditors review
4. If approved â†’ Bayar agreed amount â†’ Immediate discharge

**Example**:
```
Total debt: RM80,000
Propose VA: RM30,000 (37.5%)
Creditors accept
You bayar RM30,000 â†’ DISCHARGED!

Timeline: 3-6 bulan dari proposal to discharge
```

**Success rate**: ~60-70% kalau proposal reasonable

---

#### Cara 2: DGI Scheme (Debt Settlement Scheme)

**Eligibility**:
- Debt <RM500,000
- Bankruptcy <5 years
- Have ability to pay

**How it works**:
```
Step 1: Apply untuk DGI scheme
Step 2: DGI calculate what you can pay (based on income)
Step 3: Monthly payment to DGI untuk distribute ke creditors
Step 4: After complete payment plan (usually 3-5 years) â†’ Discharge
```

**Advantages**:
- Lower total repayment (usually 40-60% of debt)
- Faster discharge (3-5 years vs 7-10 years)
- No court involved

---

#### Cara 3: Pay Off Full Debt

**Straightforward**:
- Settle semua outstanding debt dengan DGI
- Apply untuk discharge
- Court process (2-4 bulan)
- Get Certificate of Discharge

**Where to get money untuk settle**:
- Secured loan (guna asset)
- Family assistance
- Business profit
- EPF withdrawal (under certain conditions)
