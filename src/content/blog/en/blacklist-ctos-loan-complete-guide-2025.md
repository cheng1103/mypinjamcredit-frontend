## ⚠️ 5 Kesilapan Yang MESTI Elakkan

### 1. **Apply Terlalu Banyak Loans Serentak**

**Problem:**
Setiap application akan record dalam CTOS. Jika apply 5-10 loans dalam masa singkat, semua lender akan reject sebab "desperate borrower".

**Solution:**
- Apply maksimum 2-3 lenders sahaja
- Gap minimum 2 minggu antara applications
- Focus pada lender yang specialise dalam bad credit

---

### 2. **Tidak Declare Semua Income Sources**

**Common Mistake:**
Hanya declare gaji sahaja, tidak declare side income.

**Include Semua:**
- Full-time salary
- Part-time income
- Grab/Food Panda earnings
- Online business (Shopee/Lazada)
- Rental income
- Investment dividends

**印度社区提示：**
很多印度朋友有多个收入来源（estate work + part time），但只申报主要工资。记得把所有收入都写上，这样批准机会更高！

---

### 3. **Provide Fake Documents**

**JANGAN SEKALI-KALI:**
- Fake payslips
- Edited bank statements
- Photoshop documents

**Kenapa Jangan:**
- Illegal - boleh kena tindakan undang-undang
- Blacklist permanently dengan semua lenders
- Rugi deposit jika ada

**Honest is ALWAYS the best policy!**

---

### 4. **Tidak Baca Terms & Conditions**

**Mesti Confirm:**
- ✅ Total interest rate per year
- ✅ Processing fees
- ✅ Late payment penalties
- ✅ Early settlement fees (if any)
- ✅ Total amount to repay
- ✅ Monthly instalment exact amount

**华人朋友特别注意：**
有些公司会说"利息很低"，但是加上各种fees后总成本很高。一定要看Total Amount Repayable，不是只看利率！

## 📞 FAQ - Soalan Lazim

### Q1: Bankrupt boleh apply loan tak?

**Answer:** Bergantung kepada status:
- **Undischarged Bankrupt:** Tidak boleh apply secara rasmi. Kena selesaikan bankruptcy dulu.
- **Discharged Bankrupt:** BOLEH! Selepas discharge, anda boleh apply pinjaman. Biasanya lenders akan tengok 2-3 tahun after discharge date.

---

### Q2: CCRIS merah semua, masih boleh dapat loan?

**Answer:** BOLEH, tetapi options terhad:
1. Licensed moneylender (higher interest)
2. Collateral-based loan
3. Koperasi (jika anda ahli)
4. Business loan (jika ada business)

---

### Q3: Berapa lama blacklist akan hilang dari CTOS?

**Answer:**
- **Negative payment record:** Remain for 12 months AFTER fully settled
- **Bankruptcy record:** Remain for 5 years AFTER discharge
- **Lawsuit record:** Remain for 7 years

**Important:** Even after 12 months, score tak akan automatic jadi good. Kena rebuild credit actively!

---

### Q4: Licensed moneylender interest berapa?

**Answer:**
Maximum **18% per tahun** (regulated by KPKT).

**Comparison:**
- Bank interest: 5-8% pa (untuk good credit)
- Licensed moneylender: 12-18% pa
- Loan shark: 50-200%++ pa (ILLEGAL!)
