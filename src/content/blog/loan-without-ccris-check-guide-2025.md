### âš ï¸ Kenapa People Nak Avoid CCRIS/CTOS Check?

**Common reasons**:

1. **Bad credit history**
   - Late payments dalam past
   - Loan defaults
   - Special attention accounts
   - Low CTOS score (<550)

2. **Bankruptcy record**
   - Current atau discharged bankrupt
   - Masih dalam CTOS for 7 years

3. **Too many credit enquiries**
   - Apply terlalu banyak loan dalam short period
   - Each enquiry affects score

4. **High debt service ratio**
   - Already banyak loan commitments
   - DSR >70% auto reject by bank

5. **Privacy concerns**
   - Tak nak record dalam system
   - Self-employed/business owner yang value discretion

6. **Incomplete records**
   - New to Malaysia (no credit history)
   - Never had loan/credit before
   - Foreign spouse/PR
